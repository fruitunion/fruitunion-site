Our Story

The Fruit Union was born from a simple idea: making healthy eating easy and accessible for everyone. We saw how busy life can get, and how difficult it can be to find the time to buy, wash, and prepare fresh fruit every day. So, we decided to do the work for you.

Founded in 2023, we started as a small team with a big passion for health and wellness. We partner with local farmers to select the best seasonal fruits, ensuring that what you receive is always fresh, ripe, and full of nutrients. Our dedicated team carefully prepares your fruit bowls in hygienic, eco-friendly packaging every morning, and our delivery crew ensures it reaches your doorstep just in time for breakfast.

We are more than just a delivery service; we are a community dedicated to promoting a healthier lifestyle, one delicious fruit bowl at a time.
